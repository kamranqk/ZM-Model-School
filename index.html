Who we are?

Care Community Resource Center (CCRC) was established in 2016, it is an organisation established to serve the local community, which focuses on education and training of children. The aim of this institution is to provide education to underprivileged children.

Happening @ ZM Academy

Our educational focus introduces foundational programs, including: Guided English Reading Program, Arabic and Urdu language courses, Math and Science, and Islamic Studies. We are in the process of developing unique lesson plans for all subjects for grades 6-10.
Extracurricular Activities @ ZM Academy
The Saturday is set aside for activities where we try to learn something new without reading the syllabus. And for this effort we have introduced many clubs and activities like Movie Club, Sketching, Stitching Club, Cooking Club, Physical Fitness Club, Urdu Class, Computer etc.
Counselling Services
To help students focus on their studies, we offer Individual counselling and financial assistance to those performing below average academically.
